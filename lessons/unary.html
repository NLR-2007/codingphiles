Unary Operators

Unary operators operate on a single operand.

Types of Unary Operators

✔ ++ (Increment)
✔ -- (Decrement)
✔ + (Unary plus)
✔ - (Unary minus)
✔ ! (Logical NOT)

Example

int a = 5;
a++; // a becomes 6
